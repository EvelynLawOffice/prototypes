Personal Injury

When an accident or injury disrupts your life, our firm is here to help you seek justice and fair compensation. We provide skilled representation for individuals who have been harmed due to the negligence or misconduct of others. Our personal injury services include:

  • Motor Vehicle Accidents
  • Slip and Fall Accidents
  • Workplace Accidents
  • Medical Malpractice
  • Product Liability
  • Wrongful Death
  • Insurance Claims and Disputes

Immigration Law

We provide comprehensive immigration services to help clients navigate the complex and often overwhelming U.S. immigration system. Whether you are seeking to reunite with loved ones, secure legal status, or defend against removal, we are here to provide the support and expertise you need.

  • Family-Based Immigration
  • Naturalization and Citizenship
  • Deferred Action and Humanitarian Relief
  • Removal Defense
  • Work Visas and Employment-Based Immigration
